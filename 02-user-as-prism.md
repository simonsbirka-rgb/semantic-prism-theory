### 3.2 Radical Interpretation (Davidson)

**Core idea:** Meaning is determined by the interpretive stance of the observer.

- Interpretation requires assuming the speaker is largely rational and truthful
- Meaning emerges from the interaction of interpreter and interpreted

**Connection:** The user's prism IS their interpretive stance.
